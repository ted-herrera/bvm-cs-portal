Bruno

Not a chatbot.
An intelligence layer.

Bruno is Claude under the hood — running natively across every touchpoint in the platform. From the first sales call to the 90-day retention check.

At intake
Natural conversation → structured site
Bruno extracts all client data through conversation — no forms. Fires a silent SBR market scan for every ZIP code. Income levels, competitor density, opportunity scores. Site generates in the same session.
On the rep dashboard
Pipeline intelligence on demand
Rep types "who needs follow up?" — Bruno reads the live pipeline and answers. Flags at-risk clients, suggests Close CRM actions, recommends next steps. A VA that already knows your book of business.
